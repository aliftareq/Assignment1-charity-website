Our Donation Plan

Helping-Hand-Bangladesh conducts various activities aimed at the basic needs of the people food,
clothing, housing, education and medical for survival. In order to meet the basic needs of the deprived
children, we have been conducting several regular programs including one taka meal, Iftar and seheri
distribution in Ramadan, winter and eid cloth distribution, orphanage, free education program, one taka
medical treatment, one glass of milk, ambulance service, training center, scholarship program etc.

Food for a family

Our mission is to help both the materially poor and the poor in spirit.The materially poor are served by local school, clergy and lay leaders who have been empowered and supplied with goods by Food For The Poor.The poor in spirit are renewed by their relationship with and service to the poor through our direct our team of teaching, encouragement and prayer.Ultimately, we seek to bring both benefactors and recipients to a closer union with our Lord. ANd you can be part of this.

Emergency blood supply

Through a partnership with more than 40 local hospitals, hosting more than 100 community blood drives each month and providing regular donation opportunities at our local centers, Helping-Hand-Bangladesh is proud to serve patients throughout the country. Most importantly - we couldn't do it without our volunteer blood donors. Thank you for giving the gift of life. we owe you for your support.

Shelter for a family

In 2017, Helping-Hand-Bangladesh launched the Rehabilitation 2030 initiative, which emphasizes the need for health system strengthening, and calls for all stakeholders worldwide to come together to work on different priority areas, including: improving leadership and governance; developing a strong multidisciplinary rehabilitation workforce; expanding financing for rehabilitation; and improving data collection and research on rehabilitation
